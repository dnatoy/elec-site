Electrical Reference

This is the online-based reference for the use by the Electrical Department.

Please use the symbols on top for a quick navigation between the different sections:

The Reference Tables tab. You can find the tables for sizing the conductors, grounding conductors, equipment grounding conductors, etc.

The Calculators tab. It provides a variety of the calculators for different purposes.

The References tab. Provides access to the design references.

The Lighting Resources tab. The resources related to the lighting design, including representatives contact information, lighting manufacturer references, etc.

The Power Resources tab. The resources related to the power and electrical equipment. It includes the local representatives and other references to the useful resources from major manufacturers.
